Dance

I have been dancing since the age of five when I started learning the classical dance form, kathak. I also know the dance styles of contemporary, hip-hop, bollywood. Dance gives one the freedom to use their whole body to express themselves. Dance allows the innermost emotions to flow.

Reading

Reading has and will always be my first love. Its a passion I have nurtured since childhood. I love reading fiction especially thrillers and sci-fi. Books transport you to a totally different world. Books can make your imagination race though you are sitting in one place.

Music

Music is an integral part of my life. Though I neither play any instrument nor sing, I can deeply connect to songs and the meaning that they hold. An evening spent listening to my favourite music is an evening well spent.

Travel

Travelling is a great way to visit new places as well as connect with oneself. It gives you an opportunity to spend time with family and friends. Someday I want to travel all around the world and see the beautiful places this world has.

Movies

I'm a huge movie buff. I love watching all kinds of movies especially comedies and action. Movies are a great way to relax as well as while away time (which will never come back). If there's one thing I love, its a great movie along with great company and some popcorn.

Sleep

Sleeping is my favourite activity. When there's nothing to do (or even when there's a lot to do), sleeping is the best option. Sleeping can refresh your mind and also help one think clearly. Over the years I have developed the uncanny ability to fall asleep within a matter of few seconds and under any circumstance.
